Narrative:
Wizz Air flight W64292 suffered an engine stall of engine no. 1 during the approach to Tuzla International Airport.
The engine was shut down and the aircraft landed safely.
The aircraft was ferried to Budapest on November 10 and re-entered service on November 11.
